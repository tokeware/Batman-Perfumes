In Gotham City's depths, Meer and perfumer Ahmad Raouf joined forces to create a scent embodying Batman's essence. After months of collaboration, they unveiled "Dark Knight Elixir," a blend of musk, leather, and the city's whispers. Its launch ignited a frenzy, with Gothamites clamoring for a taste of the Caped Crusader. As the fragrance spread, Meer observed with satisfaction, seeing Batman's legacy transcend rooftops and alleys. In the quiet of his mansion, he knew that even in scent, the spirit of justice would endure.
